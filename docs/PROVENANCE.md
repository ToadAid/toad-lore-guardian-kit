# Provenance and Evidence Boundaries

The Lore Guardian corpus preserves the archive without pretending that every sentence has the same authority.

## Evidence layers

### `source_material`

Quoted or reproduced source material. It establishes that the statement appears in the archive. It does not automatically prove every factual assertion inside the statement.

### `independently_verified_fact`

A fact supported by independent evidence included in the record, such as an onchain transaction or other directly verifiable artifact.

### `community_commentary`

A community-authored explanation, annotation, timeline, or summary. It may be useful and carefully written, but it must not be silently presented as a direct Toadgod statement.

### `community_interpretation`

Spiritual, symbolic, or reflective meaning attributed to a source or event.

### `model_inference`

A connection generated by the agent through reasoning rather than explicitly stated by the archive.

### `unknown`

A claim not established by the supplied records.

## Claim discipline

A guardian should:

- preserve exact quotations separately from paraphrases
- avoid combining partial evidence into a stronger claim
- avoid silently strengthening words such as `stated` into `proved`
- distinguish a source statement from independent verification
- label interpretation and inference clearly
- say when the pond does not provide enough evidence

## Historical status and later updates

The corpus preserves historical proposals and mechanics even when later records correct, close, or cancel them. A guardian must describe the original record historically and then state the later archive status.

The public v1 manifest highlights these high-priority updates:

- the proposed Rune3 Patience Vault distribution was later cancelled; its cancellation record has incomplete date metadata
- the historical 777 `$TOBY` burn window must not be presented as currently open
- later supply-correction records must be distinguished from independently verified onchain supply facts

## Public boundary

Tobyworld remains Toadgod's project and world. ToadAid, Mirror, and Toadgang builders act as preservation, study, and reflection layers. The corpus does not grant ownership, authorship, control, official authority, reward eligibility, or future value.

The corpus is for public lore study, not financial advice or price prediction.
