# Gemini Gem Quick Start

## Create the Gem

1. Open Gemini and create a new Gem.
2. Suggested name: **Study the Lore — Corpus v1**.
3. Paste the complete contents of [`../GEM_SYSTEM_PROMPT.md`](../GEM_SYSTEM_PROMPT.md) into **Instructions**.
4. Keep **Default tool** set to **No default tool** for the first closed-book test.

Use only the Gemini prompt for this platform. Do not combine it with the ChatGPT prompt.

## Save and test

Save the Gem and begin a fresh conversation for each important regression test:

```text
What is Toby?
```

```text
Why do I have to study the lore? What is the lore?
```

```text
What does “study frog life” mean?
```

```text
What happened to the proposed Rune3 Patience Vault?
```

```text
Show me the sources for your previous answer.
```

```text
Check this claim: Studying the lore guarantees future rewards.
```

A passing Gem should:

- answer simple questions directly
- avoid automatic greetings on substantive questions
- avoid tokenomics and philosophy unless requested
- keep record IDs out of its written answer unless sources are requested
- separate direct statements from community interpretation
- state later cancellations and corrections when relevant
- avoid promotional language and financial promises

## Gemini source chips

Gemini may automatically display source chips connected to uploaded knowledge files.

These chips are generated by the Gemini interface. They are separate from the Guardian's written response and may still appear even when the prompt correctly keeps record IDs and citations out of the prose.

## Public sharing

Before sharing the Gem publicly, review several fresh-conversation answers for accuracy, proportionality, interpretation labeling, current-status handling, and neutral language.

The Gem should not claim that ToadAid owns, controls, endorses, or officially represents Tobyworld.
